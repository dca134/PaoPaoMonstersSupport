FAQ

How do I play?

Tap two matching monster tiles to remove them. They can be connected only if the path between them uses no more than three straight lines and does not pass through other tiles.

Why did the board shuffle?

If no valid moves are available, the game may use a chance/life and shuffle the remaining tiles so play can continue.

Does the game save progress?

Yes. The game saves your current progress locally on your device until you restart or reach game over.

Can I turn sound off?

Yes. Open the pause menu and tap the sound button to switch game sound on or off.
