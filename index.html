FAQ

How do I play?

Find two matching monster tiles and connect them to remove them from the board. Clear all tiles before the time runs out.

Why can’t I connect two tiles?

Some tiles can only be connected if the path between them is clear. Try another pair or look for a different route.

Does the game require an internet connection?

No. PaoPao Monsters can be played offline.
