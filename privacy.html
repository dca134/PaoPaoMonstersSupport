Privacy Policy

PaoPao Monsters

Effective date: June 16, 2026

Overview

PaoPao Monsters is a colorful tile-matching puzzle game where players connect matching monsters, clear the board, and advance through changing level rules.

This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user data.

Data Collection

PaoPao Monsters does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data from users.

The app does not include user accounts, chat, advertising, analytics, tracking, payments, or user-generated content.

Local Progress

The game may save progress locally on your device so you can continue playing. This information stays on your device and is not sent to the developer or any third party.

Internet Connection

PaoPao Monsters can be played offline and does not require an internet connection for gameplay.

Children's Privacy

PaoPao Monsters does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app changes in the future. Any updates will be posted on this page.
